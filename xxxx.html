If you find any neglected, injured or abandoned animal, please take a clear picture and send it to our Telegram team. Your report can save a life.

About RRB

RRB is an animal rescue community that helps people report neglected animals and connects volunteers to rescue them.

Our Mission

  • Protect Street Animals
  • Rescue Injured Animals
  • Create Awareness
  • Build a Better Future

How To Report

  1. Take a clear photo of the animal.
  2. Share the location.
  3. Send everything to our Telegram.
  4. Our volunteers will review the report.
